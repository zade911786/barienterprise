// About Us

Manufacturing Excellence
Since 2001

Actively Operating · Pan India Delivery

Bari Enterprise, founded by Md Mushtaque, is a leading name in the plastic granules industry based in Kolkata, West Bengal. Since 2001, we have built an unwavering reputation for delivering the highest quality HDPE and LDPE granules to manufacturers across India.

Our advanced processing facility ensures every batch meets the most stringent industrial standards. From natural and milky HDPE to super-grade LDPE, our granules are engineered for consistency, peak performance, and industrial reliability.

With over two decades of expertise, we serve 300+ clients with 1000+ tons supplied every single month — making us one of the most trusted plastic granule suppliers in Eastern India.
